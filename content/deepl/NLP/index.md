# Intro

> [!cite]
> Natural language processing is a field of science and engineering focused on the development and study of automatic systems that understand and generate natural (that is, human,) languages.[^1]

Languge is a wonderful invention by human. And around the world, only human can master it and use it to communicate with each other massive information. Well how can we use computer to understand and generate human language?

**Applications**
1. Machine Translation
2. Question Answering
3. Summarization and Analysis of text
4. Speech Recognition
...

**Limitations**
1. Acquisition ability
2. Representation quality
3. Biases on gender, race, religion etc.

Let's dive into NLP! 😁
